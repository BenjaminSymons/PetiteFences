Boundary Ambiguity FAQ

Answers to questions about our intentionally ambiguous fencing solutions

Do your fences actually work?

That's the wrong question to ask! Our fences "work" as visual suggestions rather than physical barriers. They're not designed to actually stop anything - they're more of a philosophical statement on the futility of boundaries.

Can your fences contain my dog?

Only if your dog is exceptionally unobservant or has difficulty stepping over objects that are less than 15 cm tall. For most dogs, our fences serve as interesting obstacles to step over during their explorations.

Are your fences ADA compliant?

Absolutely! We're proud to say our fences meet or exceed accessibility requirements by being easily navigable by everyone. Wheelchairs, bicycles, shopping carts - they all pass over our fences effortlessly.

Can I use your fences for security?

We strongly recommend against this. Our fences provide no security whatsoever. In fact, they might even attract attention to your property as people stop to wonder why you installed such a tiny barrier. For actual security, try something from our competitors.
